About us

Help A Society Initiative is a Community Based Organisatio(CBO) based in korogocho,Kenya established in 2019
and got registered in 2020 under the ministry of Gender and Social Services in kenya.
Our offices are located in Nairobi Kenya. HASI is dedicated to build a community that sees young people as game changers who have the power
of ideas to positively impact our community with, ideas that drives a new era of development, change, growth and productivity.

Our Vision

Changing the view of the society about the young generation to view them in a different manner

Our Mission

we believe passionately in the power of ideas to change attitude,thinking and lives by fully realising the potential of young people to positively impact our society with ideas that ushers in a new era of development, change, growth and productivity that will help curb difficult situation in our society

Our plans

HASI aims to nurture,empower and transform local young people to use their talents, skills and innovative ideas to bring a positive impact to the community and be key players in the society. Our programs are youth and teenage friendly,designed to impact knowledge and participation in social,political and economic issues to enable sustainability development in the society. We promote ideas of entrepreneurship to enable them to initiate income generating activities.
